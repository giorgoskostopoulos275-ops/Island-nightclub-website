About Us

Welcome to Island — where the rhythm meets the ocean and every night feels like a true escape.
Located just outside the city, near the beach with a magnificent ocean view, Island is more than a nightclub — it’s a destination. Surrounded by the sound of waves and breathtaking scenery, we bring together tropical beauty and electrifying nightlife in one unforgettable experience.

🌺 Our Vibe

At Island, we blend the pulse of world-class DJs with the free spirit of island life. Picture palm trees glowing under neon lights, the ocean breeze in the air, and a dance floor alive with energy. Here, every night feels like a vacation under the stars.

🎶 Music & Experience

We bring you the best in:


  • Electronic Dance Music (EDM)
  • House
  • Reggaeton
  • Latin Beats
  • Live Performances

Our powerful sound system, immersive lighting, and vibrant crowd create an atmosphere that keeps the night alive until sunrise.

🍹 Signature Taste

Enjoy handcrafted tropical cocktails, premium spirits, and exclusive VIP bottle service — all designed to match the Island vibe: bold, refreshing, and unforgettable.

🌙 More Than a Nightclub

Island is not just a place you visit — it’s a place you experience. It’s where sunsets turn into celebrations, where music meets the sea, and where memories are made with a magnificent view as your backdrop. Escape the ordinary. Feel the rhythm. Welcome to Island. 🌊🔥
